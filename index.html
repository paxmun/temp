About

With tremendous success of our last five events we are all set to launch another iteration, the 6th edition of our inter-school IT-Fest, NEXUS, on 20th October, 2016 hosted by Delhi Public School, Greater Noida. An Event entirely spear-headed by students under the guidance of our experienced teachers, NEXUS'16 promises to be Bigger, Better and Bolder than all of our previous events.

With intensifying technological fronts, we promise to expose you to events that aim to put forward the best of your abilities and breed ideas that expand your horizons. An amalgamation of exquisite skills and noxious ideas, NEXUS'16 makes an attempt to reach new levels of imagination, creativity and brain-storming. With over 200+ cyber enthusiasts, we're out on an endeavor to carry forward the lineage of unparalleled thought outputs and co-curricular engagements.
